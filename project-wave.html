WAVE — A Tangible
Interaction Experiment

A mix of art and technology. WAVE visualizes and generates music with a simple wave of your hand — bridging physical gesture and digital sound.

Concept

Where Gesture Becomes Sound

WAVE explores the idea that physical movement can be a musical instrument. By tracking hand gestures in real time, the system maps spatial data to sonic parameters — pitch, rhythm, timbre — and simultaneously generates a visual representation of the sound.

The project challenges the boundary between performer and instrument, inviting anyone to become a musician without prior knowledge of music theory or technique.

Interaction

Live Gesture to Music

A slow upward sweep generates a rising melody. A rapid sideways motion triggers percussive hits. Each gesture type is mapped to a distinct sonic and visual response, creating an intuitive vocabulary of interaction.

Visual Output

Sound Made Visible

The visual layer mirrors the sonic output — generating real-time particle flows, waveforms, and colour shifts that respond to both the gesture intensity and the musical parameters produced.
