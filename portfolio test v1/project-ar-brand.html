Overview

Making Brands Come Alive

Traditional brand touchpoints — packaging, retail displays, print — are static. This project explored how augmented reality overlays can transform passive brand materials into interactive, story-driven experiences that engage users on a deeper level.

When a customer points their phone at the product, the brand world opens up — revealing product stories, animations, and contextual information layered seamlessly over the physical object.

Design System

Trigger & Overlay Design

The AR experience is built around two layers: the image trigger (what the camera recognises) and the digital overlay (what appears above it). Both were designed in tandem to ensure a seamless, coherent brand experience.

Experience

The AR Moment

The full AR reveal — from camera recognition to overlay animation — was designed to feel instant and magical. Sub-second recognition with a smooth entrance animation removes friction and creates a sense of delight.
