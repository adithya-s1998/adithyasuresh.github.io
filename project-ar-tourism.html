Overview

History, Unlocked by Your Phone

Most tourists experience heritage sites through static plaques and audio guides that struggle to compete with the visual richness of the location itself. Timeless Tales uses location-aware AR to surface contextual stories, reconstruct missing architectural elements, and surface hidden histories — exactly where they happened.

Point your phone at a monument and watch it transform: crumbled walls rebuild, historical figures appear, and layered narratives unfold in the space around you.

UX Design

Designing the App Experience

The app was designed around three primary modes: Explore (map-based discovery of nearby AR-enabled sites), Reveal (live AR camera view with overlays), and Stories (curated audio-visual narratives per location). Each mode transitions fluidly to reduce context-switching friction.

AR Experience

Bringing History Back

The AR layer uses surface tracking and geo-anchoring to position historical reconstructions accurately relative to existing structures. Crumbled ruins gain ghostly restored forms; empty spaces fill with contextual scenes from the past.
