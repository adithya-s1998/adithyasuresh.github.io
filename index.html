Who I Am

About

I'm Adithya, an experience designer working at the intersection of technology and human experience. I thrive where emerging tech meets thoughtful design.

My work spans Gen AI, game engines, AR, VR, and XR — building interactive experiences that feel intuitive, immersive, and alive. A good challenge is something I like to ponder over; I love the problem-solving part of things.

Beyond design, I'm deeply passionate about storytelling — I want to be a great storyteller and maybe make movies someday. I'm building a personal brand and exploring content creation.
